Shipping Policy

Effective Date: 26th July, 2024

1. Delivery Areas and Coverage

Our food delivery service covers all areas within India. We are committed to expanding our reach and will update our customers as new locations become available.

2. Delivery Timeframes

We offer delivery services daily from 7 AM to 11 PM. Customers can place orders at any time, and our system will allocate the earliest available delivery slot within these hours.

3. Delivery Charges

Delivery charges are calculated based on the distance between the restaurant and the delivery location, as well as the time of the day. Specific charges will be displayed at checkout before the order is confirmed.

4. Order Tracking

Customers can track their orders in real-time through our mobile application. The tracking feature provides updates on the status of the order from preparation to delivery.

5. Special Handling Instructions

If you have specific delivery instructions or special handling requests, please include them when placing your order. We will do our best to accommodate these requests, though certain restrictions may apply based on the nature of the order.

6. Delivery Partner Information

We work with both our in-house delivery team and trusted third-party delivery partners to ensure that your food arrives on time and in excellent condition. We carefully select and train our partners to uphold our standards of service and quality.

7. Delivery Issues and Customer Support

If there are any issues with your delivery, such as late arrival or incorrect items, please contact our customer support team immediately through the app or our customer service hotline. We are committed to resolving any issues promptly and to your satisfaction.

8. Return and Refund Policy

In the event of incorrect or unsatisfactory orders, please reach out to our customer support within 24 hours of receiving your order. We will review the issue and, if necessary, provide a refund or replacement. Our priority is to ensure you have a positive experience with every order.

9. Payment Options

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, and net banking. Cash on delivery (COD) is also available for selected locations. Payment options will be clearly presented at checkout.

10. Other Considerations

We strive to deliver your food under all circumstances; however, delivery times may be affected by adverse weather conditions, traffic, or other unforeseen events. During holidays and peak times, delivery slots may be limited or delayed. We appreciate your understanding and patience during these times.
